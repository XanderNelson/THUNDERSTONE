WHO WE ARE

Our roots run deep in Holyoke, Colorado, and so will our futures. Both my brother and I were born and raised here, the man making this website was born and raised here, and the man who inspired the confidence to make this company has called Holyoke home for over 13 years. Holyoke was the perfect incubator for entrepreneurs like us. For as much grief and drama it can produce, it can produce an equal amount of creativity and industriousness that no other place on earth can match. Our mission here at Thunderstone Capital is to return to the community what it has given to us, and then some. We want to make Holyoke a haven amongst the plains. A place where I can raise my children and know with certainty that this is the best place on earth for them. We want Holyoke to be described as a metaphorically modern Eden where the technologies of the cities merge with the humility and kindness of small town folk. These are our unrelenting goals that will be the filter for our investment into Holyoke.

--Bradley Cumming CEO, CFO

WHAT WE DO

Transportation and communication technologies have made the world much smaller than we ever thought possible. In the future, distance will not be a factor in determining where to conduct business or where to invest because travel time will become negligible. With this foresight, we see the Town of Holyoke as the perfect investment opportunity, not only for sentimental reasons but for growth opportunity as well. Our intention with Thunderstone Capital is to reverse the wealth extraction that has occurred to the small towns over the last century. We intend to do this by extracting the wealth back from the large cities and reinvesting it into North East Colorado and especially our hometown of Holyoke, where our headquarters will always reside. We specialize in construction and real estate at the moment, but our expertise is quickly growing.

--Bradley Cumming CEO, CFO
